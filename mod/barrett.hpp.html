Fast modular arithmetic via Barrett reduction. Used internally by DynamicModInt.

Usage

  • Barrett(mod): initialize with mod $m$
  • modulo(a): $a \bmod m$
  • mul(a, b): $a \cdot b \bmod m$

Required by

  • DynamicModInt (mod/dynamic_modint.hpp)
